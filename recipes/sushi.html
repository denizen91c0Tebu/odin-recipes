Sushi

Description

Sushi is a Japanese dish of prepared vinegared rice, usually with some sugar and salt, a variety of ingredients, such as seafood — often raw — and vegetables. Styles of sushi and its presentation vary widely, but the one key ingredient is "sushi rice", also referred to as shari, or sumeshi.

Ingredients

  • Sushi Rice
  • Protein of your choice(preferably fish)
  • Nori (seeweed)
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Prepare Rice
  2. Layer Rice on nori paper
  3. Put with and veggies down center long ways
  4. Wrap with mini tatami mat
