Pot Roast

Description

Pot roast is an American beef dish made by slow cooking a usually tough cut of beef in moist heat. Tougher cuts such as chuck steak, bottom round, short ribs and 7-bone roast are preferred for this technique.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp of Olive oil
  • beef
  • carrots
  • stalks of celery
  • beef broth

Steps

  1. preheat oven
  2. season that bad boy
  3. throw all your veggies in
  4. wrap it in about 2 pounds of tin foil
  5. lastly throw it on the traeger and let that baby ROAST
