Gelato

Description

Gelato is the common word in Italian for all kinds of ice cream. In English, it specifically refers to a frozen dessert of Italian origin. Artisanal gelato in Italy generally contains 6 to 9% butterfat, which is lower than other styles of frozen dessert.

Ingredients

  • Flavoring such as a fruit or nut, chocolate, etc
  • heavy cream
  • whole milk
  • granulated sugar
  • egg yolks

Steps

  1. whip the egg yolks with the sugar for 5 minutes
  2. bring the milk almost to a boil for 5 minutes/li>
  3. pour the hot milk slowly into the egg sugar mixture
  4. turn heat off, add cold heavy cream
  5. place in gelato container and into the freezer for 5 hours
  6. make sure every 30 minutes your breaking the top layer of the cream
